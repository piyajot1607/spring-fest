Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a registration fee?
Registration is completely free for all participants! We believe in making SpringFest accessible to everyone.
Can I participate in multiple events?
Yes! You can register for multiple events. Just make sure to check the schedule to avoid timing conflicts.
What should I bring for the events?
Bring your college ID, any equipment specific to your event (musical instruments, sports gear), and enthusiasm! Food and refreshments will be provided.
Are there accommodation facilities?
We can help arrange accommodation in nearby hostels or budget hotels. Contact us early for assistance with bookings.
What are the prizes?

How will I know if I'm selected?
You'll receive a confirmation email within 48 hours of registration. Follow-up emails with event details will be sent closer to the date.
